About Us

Welcome to AniWel (Animal Welfare Organisation and Service Provider), where compassion meets action for the well-being of our furry, feathered, and finned friends. Our journey began with a simple yet profound belief: that every living being deserves love, respect, and a chance at a happy, healthy life.


Our Mission:
At AniWel , we are driven by a relentless commitment to promoting animal welfare, advocating for their rights, and fostering a world where the bond between humans and animals is one of mutual respect and understanding. Our mission is to create a compassionate community that champions the well-being of animals through education, outreach, and direct intervention.
